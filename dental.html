Fair questions

Before you book

Do you accept my insurance?

We're in-network with most major PPO plans and happy to check your specific coverage before your visit — just ask the assistant or front desk and we'll confirm what's covered.

I'm nervous about the dentist — is that okay here?

Very common, and the whole reason we built the practice this way. We explain every step before we do it, never rush, and can pause any time you need. No judgment, ever.

Can I really get seen same-day for an emergency?

Yes — we hold same-day slots specifically for exactly this. Message the assistant describing what's going on and it'll offer the next real opening.

Do you offer payment plans for bigger treatments?

Yes, 0% financing is available for treatments like Invisalign or larger restorative work — the assistant can walk you through options before you commit to anything.
