Fair questions

Before you sit down

Do you take walk-ins?

We keep a couple of walk-in slots open daily, but chairs book out fast — messaging ahead for a real slot beats hoping one's free when you show up.

Can I request a specific barber?

Yes — tell the assistant who you'd like and it'll check that barber's real schedule, not just the shop's general availability.

What's your cancellation policy?

Life happens — just message us more than 2 hours out and we'll rebook you free. Same-day no-shows may be asked for a small deposit next time.

Do you cut kids' hair?

Absolutely — same chairs, same barbers, patient with the wiggly ones. Ask for a kids' slot and we'll pace the appointment accordingly.
